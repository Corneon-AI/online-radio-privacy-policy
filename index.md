# Privacy Policy for Online Radio – Live FM & Music

**Last updated:** September 2026

Online Radio – Live FM & Music (“we”, “our”, “the app”) respects your privacy.  
This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We **do not collect any personally identifiable information** from users.

This includes, but is not limited to:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Physical location
- Contacts
- Messages
- Payment or financial data

---

## 2. Automatically Collected Information

The app may process **non-personal technical information** required for normal operation, such as:
- Network connectivity status
- Device audio output state
- App crash logs generated by the Android operating system

This information:
- Is **anonymous**
- Is **not linked to any user identity**
- Is **not stored by us on our servers**

---

## 3. Radio Streams & Third-Party Content

Online Radio – Live FM & Music provides access to radio streams hosted by **third-party broadcasters**.

- We do **not own, host, or control** radio content
- Stream availability depends on the broadcaster
- Third-party broadcasters may have their own privacy policies

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We **do not sell, share, or transfer** user data to third parties.

No personal or sensitive user data is shared with advertisers, analytics providers, or external organizations.

---

## 5. Data Security

- The app uses secure internet connections (HTTPS where available)
- No personal data is stored or transmitted by our servers
- We take reasonable measures to ensure secure access to radio streams

## 6. Children’s Privacy

This app is **not specifically designed for children under the age of 13**.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
