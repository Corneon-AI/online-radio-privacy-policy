# Privacy Policy – Online Radio: Live FM & Music

**Last updated:** September 2026

Online Radio – Live FM & Music (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) respects your privacy.  
This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We do **not** knowingly collect, request, or store any personally identifiable information from users.

This includes, but is not limited to:

- Name  
- Email address  
- Phone number  
- Physical location  
- Contacts  
- Messages  
- Payment or financial information  

The app does **not** require user accounts, login, or registration.

---

## 2. Automatically Processed Information

For normal operation, the app may process limited, non-personal technical information, such as:

- Network connectivity status (required for streaming radio)
- Device audio output state
- System-generated crash or error logs

This information:

- Is anonymous  
- Is not linked to any user identity  
- Is not accessed, stored, or retained on our own servers  

---

## 3. Radio Streams & Third-Party Content

Online Radio – Live FM & Music provides access to radio streams hosted by third-party broadcasters.

- We do **not** host, own, or control any radio stream content  
- Stream availability depends on the respective broadcaster  
- We do **not** share any user data with broadcasters  
- Third-party broadcasters may have their own privacy policies governing their services  

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.

- No personal or sensitive user data is shared with advertisers  
- No analytics providers or external organizations receive user data  

---

## 5. Data Security

- The app uses secure internet connections (HTTPS where available)  
- No personal user data is stored by the app  
- Reasonable technical measures are used to ensure secure access to radio streams  

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Online Radio – Live FM & Music is a general-audience application and is not specifically targeted at children under the age of 13.

We do **not** knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 7. Advertisements

Online Radio – Live FM & Music does **not** display third-party advertisements.

If advertising is added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
